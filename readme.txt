<h5><strong>Working with WoCommerce</strong></h5>
If you have installed WoocOmemrce on your WordPress website, you will see WooCommerce settings options right below Settings in administration.

<img src="https://likes-and-share.inchoweb.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/2019-10-16_2127.png?_c=1571565809" alt="2019-10-16_2127.png?_c=1571565809" />

But, first, you have to select product post type from the Settings page<strong><img src="https://likes-and-share.inchoweb.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/2019-10-16_2129.png?_c=1571565809" alt="2019-10-16_2129.png?_c=1571565809" /></strong>

Now you can go to the WooCommerce settings page.

Sometimes depending on theme maybe some positions are not available because actions of positions are removed by theme.

So if you set up everything and you don’t see anything on a single or archive page (if you choose to show likes and social share buttons on archive page) just try another position.

On most themes, these actions are not removed and we just want you to know about possible issues.
